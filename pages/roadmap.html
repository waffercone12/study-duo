Dual Roadmaps

Parallel learning syllabus checkpoints tracking the monthly milestones for GATE DA 2027 and CEED 2027.

GATE DA Timeline

Data Science & Artificial Intelligence

Month 1

Foundations Prep

  • Python coding basics (Syntax, functions, file handlers)
  • Linear Algebra vector spaces, SVD decomposition
  • Probability models & standard distributions
Month 2

Core CS & DBMS

  • Data structures stacks, queues, and binary search trees
  • Asymptotic analysis, greedy algorithms, sorting
  • ER schemas, SQL triggers, normal forms database
Month 3

Machine Learning & AI

  • Supervised Classifiers, decision trees, support vector nets
  • Heuristics search algorithms, Logic formulations
  • Sub-topic syllabus revisions & formula summaries logs
Month 4

Mock Tests & PYQs

  • Complete length Mocks mock exams with metrics checks
  • Solving IIT past papers and sample question guides
  • Identify weaker zones and adjust details before exams

CEED Timeline

Common Entrance Examination for Design

Month 1

Visual Drafting

  • Perspective rendering grids (1-point, 2-point focus)
  • Everyday life objects linework textures & shadow forms
  • Observation skills, finding variations and fonts styles
Month 2

Design Cognition

  • Abstract combinations & creative concept generations
  • User journeys maps, signifiers, and ergonomic cases
  • Part B layout problem solving and storyboard outlines
Month 3

Design Awareness

  • Design histories, Masters list, famous products tags
  • Previous CEED Part A and Part B papers evaluations
  • General environment, sustainability, Indian crafts
Month 4

Model Worksheets

  • Regular timed mock tests logs & portfolio reviews
  • Speed sketching guidelines for complex objects
  • Feedback correction iterations and formula scans study
